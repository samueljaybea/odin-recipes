Traditional Ragu Bolognese

Description

Super delicious and simple traditional ragu bolognese recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil
  • 1kg Beef Mince
  • 700g Passata
  • 3-5 Bay Leaves
  • 2 Medium-large onions
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 Celery sticks
  • 2 Cups of Wine (optional)
  • 2 Cups whole milk
  • Pasta Of Your Choice (penne and tagiatelle work well, spaghetti is undesirable)
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps

  1. Saute onion, carrot and celery in a generous amount of olive oil at least until onion is transparent. (longer the better, small burnt bits add flavour)
  2. Add mince, season with salt and pepper, cook until browned and excess water is cooked out. (again burnt bits add flavour)
  3. Add wine, cook off alcohol and reduce.
  4. Add passata, bay leaves, and give an initial seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Fill empty Passata glass with water and add that to allow for evaporation.
  6. Cook for at least 2-3 hours, adding water as needed
  7. Add whole milk, and simmer for another 5 minutes, then turn off the heat
  8. Do a final seasoning to fine tune to your taste
  9. Cook your pasta to packet directions
  10. Add pasta and bolognese to saucepan and cook together for a few minutes to combine.
  11. Enjoy!
